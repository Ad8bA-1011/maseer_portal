# Privacy Policy - Maseer Media Portal

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

Maseer Media Portal is a static GitHub Pages site that facilitates registration for AI video marketing services. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Brand Name**: Your business name (required)
- **Industry**: Business category (required)
- **Colors**: Brand color preferences (required)
- **Contact**: WhatsApp number (optional)
- **Engagement Data**: Form interaction patterns via `consciousness.js` (anonymous)

### What We Do NOT Collect
- No cookies are stored
- No server-side logs (static site)
- No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No personal identification beyond business contact

## Data Storage

All registration data is sent directly to **GitHub Issues** in the `maseer_automation` repository:
- Data is stored in GitHub's infrastructure
- Subject to [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)
- Logo files are stored in the repository's `logos/` directory

## Temporary Storage (sessionStorage)

We use `sessionStorage` (browser-only, cleared on tab close) for:
- Tracking registration flow between pages
- Storing brand name during multi-step process
- **Duration**: Cleared automatically after 5 minutes or when tab closes
- **No tracking across sessions**

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| GitHub | Issue creation | Registration form data |
| Telegram | Video delivery | Brand name, video files |
